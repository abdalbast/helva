WHAT_HELVA_IS
Helva is an AI product company focused on language. It builds purpose-built
AI products — each with its own fine-tuned model, interface, and evaluation
loop — for underserved languages and UK-compliant AI.

PRODUCTS
- Deqnus: AI-powered Kurdish–English translation. Sorani-first, dialect-aware. Status: Live. URL: https://deqnus.vercel.app
- Helva Chat: Multilingual AI assistant for UK regulatory and cultural context. GDPR-compliant, transparent reasoning. Status: In development.

SOLUTIONS
- Language workflows: document translation, real-time multilingual chat, custom model fine-tuning, API integration
- Trusted UK AI: GDPR-compliant deployment, data residency, audit trails, compliance documentation
- Partner pilots: structured pilot programmes, domain model co-development, evaluation & benchmarking, research partnerships

THESIS
Most AI ignores most languages. The world's AI infrastructure is built for
English. Helva exists to close that gap — not with general-purpose models,
but with purpose-built products that treat every language as a first-class citizen.
